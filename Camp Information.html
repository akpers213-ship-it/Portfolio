Five Weeks of Fun!

We have five weeks of camp open and sign ups fill up quick! Look at all of the options below to check and see what fits best for you!

Full Day Camps

An $85 nonrefundable deposit is required with registration to reserve your spot.  This goes towards your total cost of $350.  We provide healthy and balanced lunches as well as morning and afternoon snacks during the day.  You'll receive a confirmation packet with your remaining balance in the mail upon registering.

Join Now

Half Day Camps

A $40 nonrefundable deposit is required with registration to reserve your spot. This goes towards your total cost of $150. You bag a small meal and we provide two snacks during the camp. You'll receive a confirmation packet with your remaining balance in the mail upon registering.
